Introduction

What is this course about?

The aim of this course is to teach good practices in informatics to scientists whose primary field is not copmuter science.

Who is teaching this course?

We are researchers in biology who learnt about informatics on the way, as programming skills were becoming necessary for our research projects.

Who can attend this course?

There is no need for prior experience with programming to attend the course.
